Ahman Green Rules
Quick rundown of how to play.

Objective

  • First player to hit Black → White → Green → Red (in that order) wins.

Turns & darts

  • Each player gets 3 darts per turn.
  • For each dart, tap the color you hit for the current player.
  • Out-of-order colors don’t advance progress, but still use a dart.

Special cases

  • Reset: Miss the board completely → send the player back to needing Black.
  • Party: Tiny rim hit (inside the outer ring but not a scoring segment) → jump the player straight to needing Red.

Winning

  • As soon as a player completes Red after doing Black, White, and Green in order, they immediately win the game.
